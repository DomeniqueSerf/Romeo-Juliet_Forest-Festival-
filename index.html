About

The Romeo and Juliet forest festival is a real world,dark fairytale experience brought to life by tenebrous hip hop sounds and ghost like dancers in a hidden forest. Inspired by the intense connection of a vibrant love at first site and the dark consequences of their unforbidden romance. Festival goers will experience the beautiful,haunting love story of Romeo and Juliet like never before.

Community

Don't be deluded by whimsical dreams, this physical world is not what it seems, and when the darkness falls and the earth starts to call, who will stand tall to protect it all?

We need to be there when earth calls in despair, she's our love child, meant to be wild and free, our mission is to offer new ways to experiencing art while making a difference in the environments we chant.

For each ticket sold festival goers will be donating to the ‘Let it grow fund’ allowing us to improve air quality and protect the natural wildlife population in Silver strand falls. For art might be small but we have the power to conserve the beauty of it all.
